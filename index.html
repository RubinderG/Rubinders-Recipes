Roasted Garlic Potatoes

These crispy, golden oven-roasted potatoes are lightly seasoned with garlic and paprika, they are a perfect side dish with any meal. 😋

I make these potatoes at least once a week, and while it's not a complicated recipe, it's one I really wanted to share with you all. You need very few ingredients to make these and the majority of the time will have these lying around in your kitchen cupboards. So, why not try to make these this week?
